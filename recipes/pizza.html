Simple Breakfast Pizza

Description

Simple breakfast pizza that is truly for breakfast. You can also put any additional toppings you would like. It is also great with ham diced up real small

ingredients

  • 5 plain bagels, split
  • 1 (14 ounce) package cooked bacon, cut into 1/4-inch pieces
  • 1 cup prepared sausage gravy
  • 1 (8 ounce) package shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Arrange bagels onto a baking sheet with the cut sides up. Arrange bacon pieces atop the bagel halves. Spoon gravy lightly atop the bacon. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese over the gravy.
  3. Bake in preheated oven until the cheese melts, about 10 minutes.
